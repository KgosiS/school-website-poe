About Us

BookWings is a non-profit organization dedicated to igniting a love for reading in young hearts by providing them with the gift of books. Our mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of their background or circumstances, has access to the magical world of literature. Through generous donations and community support, BookWings collects and distributes a wide range of age-appropriate books to kids in need. Our dedicated volunteers work tirelessly to organize book drives, source diverse and engaging titles, and collaborate with local schools, libraries, and community centers to reach children across the region. By fostering a culture of literacy and imagination, BookWings aims to empower these young minds to dream big, learn, and grow through the transformative power of books. Join us in spreading the joy of reading, one book at a time, and help us give every child the wings to soar into a brighter future.
